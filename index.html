Your Opening Story — 3 Versions

Pick one depending on what they ask. Practise out loud tonight.

When they ask: "Tell me about yourself"
"I'm a Senior Technical Lead at EY with 7 years in enterprise integration. I've spent the last 3 years leading a BizTalk-to-Azure migration for a large Insurance client — Logic Apps Standard, Service Bus, Functions, APIM, all with private networking and Managed Identity. Before that I did deep BizTalk work at Accenture. I also hold AZ-204 and have built Azure AI enrichment pipelines using Azure Cognitive Services."
When they ask: "Have you worked with Service Bus / APIM / Event Hub?"
"Yes — on the Insurance project at EY. We used Service Bus Premium with topics and subscriptions to fan out policy events to billing, CRM, and portal services. APIM sat in front with JWT validation, rate limiting, and a managed identity policy that published directly to Service Bus — no relay function needed. I set up DLQ monitoring with Azure Monitor alerts and wrote KQL queries in Log Analytics to trace messages end-to-end."
When they ask about your biggest technical challenge
"Migrating 23 BizTalk orchestrations without disrupting the live Insurance platform. We ran Azure and BizTalk in parallel for 6 weeks, automated semantic output comparison between the two systems, and did a dark-launch cutover with zero production incidents. The hardest part was the ordered processing — BizTalk correlation sets became Service Bus sessions, and I had to redesign 4 complex orchestrations into Durable Functions fan-out patterns."
